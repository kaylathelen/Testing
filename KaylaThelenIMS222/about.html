Our Story

Founded in 2016, Lakeview Luxe Boutique has been dedicated to providing the latest fashion trends and timeless pieces to our beloved community. Our founder, Kayla Thelen, envisioned a space where women could find unique clothing and accessories that express their individuality.

Lakeview Luxe began as Kayla Thelen’s vision to create a boutique that felt more like a sanctuary for personal style than just another shop. Kayla’s passion for fashion goes beyond clothing; she’s committed to making every visitor feel welcome and inspired, with staff who are dedicated to helping clients find pieces that truly suit them. More than just a place to shop, Lakeview Luxe is a space where style, community, and quality come together, honoring the unique tastes and needs of each individual who walks through the door.

What We Offer

At Charming Boutique Chicago, we believe in quality and style. Our collections are carefully selected to offer:

  • Fall Collection– Cozy, chic styles designed to keep you warm and stylish throughout the season.
  • New Christmas Collection – Festive and elegant pieces perfect for the holiday season, ideal for gifting or adding a seasonal flair to your wardrobe.
  • Handcrafted Accessories – Unique, artisan-made bags, scarves, and belts that offer a one-of-a-kind touch to your look.
  • Women’s Clothing – A curated range of stylish and timeless apparel, from dresses and tops to skirts and outerwear, tailored to elevate your everyday style.
